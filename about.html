About Us

At Popper's, our mission is not to tutor kids or be just another after-school activity. We aim to demonstrate to both students and their parents that anyone can engage in science with a meaningful impact. We believe that the problems of science are everyone's problems and are worthy of your time. As Feynman put it, "the pleasure of finding out" is our goal, which is too often forgotton in adulthood.

Our group teaches the necessary skills for students to excel. We are not a school; instead, we take the skills taught in school and show students just how much they can truly achieve. Since we are connected to Universities, we can potentially facilitate collaborations between students and active research labs, providing real-world scientific experiences with real-world impact.
